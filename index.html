QUAIL LONDON

Our founder Charlie appeared on MasterChef in 2012. He then worked at Heston Blumenthal's 'Dinner' before developing his own range of Asian-inspired canapés.

We have completed over three hundred events for some of the world's leading galleries, fashion retailers, drinks brands, charities, schools and intimate private parties.

Based in Chiswick, we work with clients in London and further afield.

Our seasonal menus focus on bold colours and presentation whilst often incorporating Middle and Far Eastern flavours. We provide canapés, bowl food, brunch and more formal private dining that can all be paired with our drinks menu.
